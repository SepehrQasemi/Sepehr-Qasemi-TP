Featured Project - ATA CRM

B2B CRM for food products and additives trading

ATA CRM is a full-stack SaaS project designed for a company working with suppliers and customers in food ingredients and additives. The system centralizes contacts, companies, products, categories, leads, tasks, and email operations with role-based behavior.

Open Live Demo Open CRM Repository Open Deliverables Hub

Architecture and Core Modules

Technical and product coverage

Stack

Next.js 16, Supabase (PostgreSQL + Auth + RLS), Brevo, Vercel, GitHub Actions.

Operational Modules

Leads, tasks, contacts, companies, products, categories, colleagues, and notifications.

Email and Automation

Manual send, role-based analytics, template use, and admin-protected automation jobs.

Pipeline Logic

Stage transitions, negotiation decisions, and weighted forecast model by stage probability.

Data Access Model

Role-based behavior for admin, manager, commercial, and standard users.

Quality

Coverage and E2E gates with CI-based validation before release and demo use.

Demo Flow

Compact instructor walkthrough
  1. Sign in and verify role-specific navigation.
  2. Create or open a company profile, then attach relevant products.
  3. Open a contact profile linked to the target company.
  4. Create a lead with source and assignment rules.
  5. Move lead stages to negotiation, then mark won or lost.
  6. Create tasks and show calendar impact.
  7. Send manual email and review analytics scope.
  8. Open dashboard KPI and weighted forecast.
  9. Show documentation proof from deliverables links.
